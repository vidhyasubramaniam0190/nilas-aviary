About Us

We Love Birds, Just Like You

Nila's Aviary was founded with a simple passion — bringing joy to families through beautiful, healthy, and well-socialised pet birds. Every bird in our collection is raised with love and expert care.

We specialise in hand-raising baby birds so they become trusting, friendly companions. All birds are health-checked, dewormed, and ready for their forever homes.
